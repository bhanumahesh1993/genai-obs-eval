Acme Support publishes a clear refund policy for digital purchases. Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of the original charge. After 30 days, refunds require manager review. Billing disputes should be escalated to the finance queue with the order id attached. Password resets never ask for the old password; customers use the secure link in account settings. Shipping delays longer than five business days qualify for a goodwill coupon.
